Project 3 – CNC Certification Project

Overview

This project served as a hands-on introduction to CNC machining as part of the HAAS CNC certification. The goal was to design and machine a personalized aluminum coaster.

Key Operations

  • CAM programming in Fusion 360
  • 2.5D pocketing and contour toolpaths
  • Finishing with a 1/8” ball end mill

Results

Dimensional accuracy within ±0.0015”, and a surface finish of Ra ≤ 32 μin. The final design was anodized and laser-engraved.
