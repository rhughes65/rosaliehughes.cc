Hand Machined Clock

Project 1 – MECH200 Fabrication Project

Home Projects Contact

Overview

This project involved machining a fully functional aluminum clock using only manual equipment — including a mill, lathe, and laser cutter. Every feature was cut and fitted by hand without CNC assistance.

Tools Used

  • Bridgeport vertical mill
  • South Bend engine lathe
  • Laser cutter (for clock face and detailing)

Process

The base, gears, and housing were machined from 6061 aluminum. Tolerances of ±0.002” were maintained on rotating components for proper alignment and smooth motion.
